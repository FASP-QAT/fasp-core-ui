Managing Freight Costs, Lead Times, and Planning Unit Prices

This screen allows you to manage specific details such as freight costs, lead times, and planning unit prices for procurement agents and planning units within your supply plan program.

Adding or Editing Rows

To add a row, follow these steps:

  • Program, Planning Unit, and Procurement Agent: Fill in these fields.
  • Creating a Procurement Agent/Planning Unit Combination: Select options from both the planning unit and procurement agent columns.
  • Procurement Agent-Specific Row Without Tying to a Planning Unit: Choose ‘All’ in the planning unit column.
  • Freight costs, lead times, and unit price: At least one of the remaining columns must have a numeric value.
Constraints and Rules
  • Only one row is allowed per program/planning unit/procurement agent combination.
  • If a procurement agent row includes ‘All’ planning units, the unit price column will be non-editable.
Usage in Supply Planning Module

These details will impact your supply planning module as follows:

  • Freight Percentages: Prioritized by program/procurement agent/planning unit settings and then program settings.
  • Lead Times: Prioritized by program/procurement agent/planning unit settings, then realm level procurement agent settings, and finally program settings.
  • Unit Prices: Prioritized by program/procurement agent/planning unit settings, then realm level procurement agent settings, and finally program settings.
